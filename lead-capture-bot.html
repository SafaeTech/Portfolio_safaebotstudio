Lead Generation Bot - Product Overview

What is the Lead Generation Bot? The Lead Generation Bot is a smart, automated assistant that helps businesses collect and manage customer leads effortlessly. It engages visitors by prompting them to leave their email address to receive more information about your product or service.

Key Features - Captures customer email through a friendly conversation flow. - Stores each email automatically in a connected Google Sheet. - Sends a confirmation email to every user upon submission. - Enables personalized email marketing and follow-ups. - Includes an easy opt-out option for user privacy.
