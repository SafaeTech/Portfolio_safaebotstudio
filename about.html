What Is a Chatbot? And Why It Matters in 2025

What Is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is a virtual assistant that interacts with users via messaging platforms or websites — answering questions, guiding purchases, booking appointments, and more.

Thanks to AI and NLP (Natural Language Processing), today’s bots can:

  • 💬 Understand natural human language
  • ⏱️ Provide real-time answers 24/7
  • 🔁 Automate repetitive tasks
  • 📈 Improve customer satisfaction and engagement

Why Chatbots Matter in 2025

  • ✅ 80% of customers expect immediate responses
  • ✅ Small businesses need to compete smart, not just hard
  • ✅ Chatbots reduce costs and increase conversion rates
  • ✅ Multilingual support opens global opportunities
  • ✅ Integrated bots boost SEO and site performance
